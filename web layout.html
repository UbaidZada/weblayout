WELCOME TO DAILY BURJ KHALIFA

The Burj Khalifa Arabic pronunction (Khalifa Tower), known as the Burj Dubaiprior to its
inauguration 2010,is a skyscraper in Dubai ,United Arab Emirates. It is known for being the
world's tallest building. With a total height of 829.8m(2,722 ft, or just over half a mile) and a
roof height (excluding antena, but including a 223 m spire) of 828 m (2,717 ft), the Burj Khalifa
has been the tallest structure and building in the world since its topping out in 2009.

Construction And Structure


The tower was constructed by Samsung C&T rom South Korea, which

And Arabic from the UAE. Hong Kong-based Far East Aluminum
